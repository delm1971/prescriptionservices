The Prescription Cost Problem

For many Americans, prescription medication costs are a major monthly expense. People managing diabetes, heart health, asthma, arthritis, blood pressure, or other long-term conditions may need recurring refills every month.

Because prices can vary, more consumers are now comparing medication options before refilling prescriptions.

The smart move is no longer just filling a prescription automatically — it is comparing options first.
